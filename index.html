Randy De Lucia

Italian country rock & rock’n’roll musician, songwriter and producer

Songs from dust, steel and long roads.

Randy De Lucia is an Italian country rock and rock’n’roll musician and songwriter, and producer, active since 2001. Born in La Spezia, he began playing guitar at an early age and developed a background spanning country, rockabilly and rock. He publishes his music independently through CD Baby and is present on major digital platforms including Spotify, YouTube and Apple Music. Alongside his solo project, he has worked as an arranger and sound engineer for various musical projects. His work reflects a strong technical background combined with an authorial and experimental approach.
